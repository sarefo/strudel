## Setting speed

+ setcpm() - counts per minute
+ setcps() - counts per second 

## Sound banks

+ numbers soundbank only from 0-8
+ 

## Stacking sounds

+ `stack(…)` - plays sounds at same time
+ can also use `$: …` (`_$:`to mute a voice)
 
## Playing samples

+ `n("x y …").bank(…)` - plays sample numbers x,y,… from bank
